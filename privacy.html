Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Access

With your authorization, VideoMate accesses the following TikTok data:

  • Profile information (username, avatar, bio)
  • Profile statistics (follower count, following count, likes, video count)
  • Video list (your published TikTok videos)
  • Video upload capability (to post drafts or publish videos)

2. How We Use Your Data

All data accessed is used solely to display information within your dashboard and to upload videos on your behalf. We do not store, share, or sell your TikTok data.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored temporarily in your browser session only. No user data is stored on our servers. Tokens are cleared when you log out.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with any third parties. The App uses only TikTok's official APIs.

5. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at privacy@videomate.app.
