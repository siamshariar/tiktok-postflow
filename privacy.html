VideoMate Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Introduction

VideoMate ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

2. Information We Access

With your explicit authorization, VideoMate accesses:

  • Profile Information: Display name, avatar, Open ID, bio, profile link, verification status
  • Profile Statistics: Follower count, following count, total likes, video count
  • Video Content: List of published videos and video upload capability

3. How We Use Information

All accessed data is used exclusively to:

  • Display personalized dashboard content
  • Enable video upload and publishing functionality
  • Show profile analytics to the authenticated user

4. Data Storage

We do NOT store your personal data, videos, or profile information on our servers. Access tokens are temporarily stored in your browser session and are automatically cleared upon logout.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, rent, or share your information with third parties. No data is used for advertising, analytics tracking, or marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

Access tokens are stored only in the user's browser session storage. Communication with APIs uses HTTPS encryption.

7. User Rights

You may revoke access at any time by logging out or removing the App from your authorized applications. Logging out immediately clears all session data.

8. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Session storage is used only for maintaining authentication state.

9. Third-Party Links

Our App integrates with official APIs. Please review their privacy policies for information on their data practices.

10. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.

12. Contact

For privacy concerns: privacy@videomate.app
